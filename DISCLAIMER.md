## Results are not the thesis's results

Every experiment here runs at a **deliberately reduced scale** so it trains on a
consumer laptop — far smaller than the thesis (fewer grids, load scenarios, model
parameters, and training samples). **The numbers in this repository are the results of
this reduced-scale reproduction, not the results reported in the thesis.** They are
provided for learning and illustration and **must not be cited as, or confused with,
the thesis's published results.** Where this project's findings diverge from the
thesis, that divergence is documented honestly as a finding of the small-scale setup —
not a claim about the original work.
